About KCO - Kangkong Chips Original

Kangkong Chips Original (KCO) is more than just a snack brand—it’s a story of passion, innovation, and a love for Filipino flavors. Founded by Josh Mojica at just 17 years old, KCO started as a small idea and grew into a recognized name in the food industry. With a vision to offer a healthier alternative to traditional chips, KCO proudly brings you crispy, delicious, and nutrient-rich kangkong chips, made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing high-quality, flavorful, and healthy snack options. Our goal is to promote sustainable and natural food choices while supporting local farmers and communities.

Our Vision

To become a household name in the Philippines and beyond, bringing innovative and delicious Filipino snacks to every home.

Josh Mojica - Founder & Innovator

Why KCO?

What sets KCO apart is our commitment to quality and authenticity. We ensure that every bag of kangkong chips is packed with flavor, crunch, and goodness. Our snacks are perfect for those who want to enjoy something crispy and tasty—without the guilt!
